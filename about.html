About Me

Dr. Karthikeyan R is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Commerce at PSG College of Arts & Science (Autonomous), Coimbatore. His academic and research interests span Finance, Banking, Capital Markets, AI in Commerce, Digital Economy and Sustainable Business Practices. He actively contributes to scholarly research through journal publications, book chapters, conferences, FDPs and interdisciplinary academic engagements.
